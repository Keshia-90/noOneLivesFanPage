About No One Lives

No One Lives is a horror/thriller film. It is based around a wealthy couple who are travelling across country. While the couple are travelling, a gang of cold blooded highway killers kidnap them. Once they have been kidnapped, the gang make a shocking discovery that things are not as they appear.



My Opinion

I personally loved this film. As a fan of films with loads of gore and action, No One Lives definitley fits into this category. The plot twist of this film is rather awesome as it's pretty much a case of the hunters becoming the hunted.
